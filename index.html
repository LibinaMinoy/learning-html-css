MIND EMPOWERED

ME has devised a holistic empowerment program to focus on Psychological and Social Empowerment of youth and women.


  • Develop a positive mind-set in the youth
  • Encourage them to express their true feelings and reach out when in need

To educate people regarding mental health. To Change the public attitude and Remove the taboo associated with mental health To Urge people to reach out and take help from a support group like ME. To Improve physical wellbeing by Yoga, Zumba and Self-defense techniques.

ME Solution

  1. ME provides you with a platform to interact with mental health experts from all around the globe for free.
  2. Instead of clearing your doubts via google, which may not always be a reliable source, you can get in touch with these professionals every Saturday 4:00pm to 5:00pm for free.
  3. Online sessions can be attended from the comfort of your homes.

ME floats a Google form periodically wherein you are free to anonymously send in any queries. Our panel doctors answer these queries during the month-end sessions.
